About Us.

KAFIE IS A DEVELOPMENT IDEA THAT WE INTEND TO ANSWER THE PROBLEMS IN THE LEARNING SECTOR SUCH AS:

  • Helping the teachers to get a job.
  • creating the platform that connect teachers and people who want him.

The idea of KAFIE created by group of students from HargaBits when they Seeing the problem in the field of education especially for parents to find a good and experienced teacher to teach their children using online and offline.By solving this, we have created a platform that meets the teacher and the parent or the principles while the teacher's information is entered into the platform after that the parent/administrator should chooses the teacher they want.
